IIM Bangalore Highlights 2024

Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB) is a leading graduate school of Management in Asia. IIMB has been ranked 2nd among the top Management institutes by NIRF 2023 and 31st in the Masters in Management category by the QS World University Ranking 2024. In addition, the institute has also been ranked 3rd by Business Today ranking 2023.
Established in 1973, IIMB is famous for its excellent faculty members who have studied and taught at some of the best universities in India and abroad. The courses offered at IIM Bangalore include Certificate, Postgraduate, and PhD programmes in the Management stream. During IIM Bangalore placements 2023, a total of 606 offers were made to PGP and PGP BA. IIM Bangalore average package stood at INR 35.31 LPA. IIM Bangalore course admission is based on the entrance exams, namely CAT, GATE, etc. IIM Bangalore cutoff is based on scores obtained in the CAT exam, followed by WAT, GD and PI.
IIM Bangalore has 150+ faculty members, about 1,200 students across various degree-granting programmes, and nearly 5,000 annual Executive Education participants. IIM Bangalore has key partnerships and collaborations with Goldman Sachs and Kotak Bank for women entrepreneurship, with the Michael and Susan Dell Foundation for incubation of non-profits, and with NITI Aayog for scaling up new ventures. Q: What is the rank of IIM Bangalore for MBA?

A: IIM Bangalore is a prestigious management institute in India. It has been ranked by QS World University Ranking 2024 at 31st under the Masters in Management category.It ranks 2nd in NIRF under Management

Q: Is IIM Bangalore good for MBA?

A: Getting an MBA from an IIM in India, IIM Bangalore or any other is undoubtedly worthwhile. Considered among the top B-schools in India for Management (PGP) education, IIMs carry a brand name and reputation which is competitive even in the international education industry. Moving to IIM Bangalore, IIM-B is the second-best IIM and Management institute in the country as per NIRF report 2023.

Q: Why choose IIM Bangalore executive MBA course?

A: With an average package of INR 33.07 LPA and an NIRF ranking of 2 for its Management courses, E-MBA from IIMs is considered one of the best choices for working professionals who want to improve their knowledge and education level by pursuing a Management degree. Additionally, IIM Bangalore has seen an improving trend in the average package over the years. In 2022, the average package stood at INR 30.09 LPA and INR 26.08 LPA in 2021.

Q: What is the selection process for IIM Bangalore MBA?

A: Candidates selected for MBA course based on their Class 10, Class 12, CAT qualifying and CAT final cutoff are called for further selection rounds, such as the Written Ability Test and Personal Interview. A commutative score is then calculated based on the performance of the candidates in all the aforementioned parameters. Similarly for other courses, applicants who meet the set eligibility need to go through the institute-specific selection process and get shortlisted. After completion of the selection rounds, a category-wise merit list is released based on the Final Composite Score. The provisional admission offers are made accordingly, adhering to the reservation norms. Students who are willing to take admission now must pay the admission fee and confirm their seat.

IIM Bangalore Admissions Process 2024

IIM Bangalore admissions are completely entrance-based. The institute offers MBA, Executive MBA, and other courses at PG, doctoral and certificate level courses in the field of Management. Candidates seeking admission at IIM Bangalore are required to appear for national-level or college-level entrance exams, according to the selected course. Admission to the flagship course, i.e.IIM Bangalore MBA is based on a valid score in the CAT exam, followed by a Personal Interview (PI) and Written Ability Test (WAT) round. IIM Bangalore admission to Indian students is offered via CAT and the international students need to register for GMAT or GRE. IIM Bangalore Cutoff 2023

IIM Bangalore cut off for PG course MBA/ PGDM(4 courses)is based on CAT scores. The institute releases two types of CAT cutoffs, namely the Qualifying CAT cutoff and the Final CAT cutoff. Securing a minimum qualifying cutoff just means that the candidate is eligible for the next steps in the admission procedure. Also, IIM Bangalore final CAT cutoff is always higher than the qualifying cutoff. The Qualifying cutoffs for IIMs mostly remain the same every year. For IIM Bangalore admissions 2024, the Qualifying CAT cutoff is set at 85 Percentile, while the sectional cutoffs are established between 80 Percentile and 75 Percentile for the General category candidates. The Qualifying CAT cutoffs are released for other category candidates as well.

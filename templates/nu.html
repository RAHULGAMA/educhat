NALSAR Highlights 2024

The National Academy of Legal Studies and Research, also known as NALSAR University of Law is a National Law University located in Secunderabad, Hyderabad. The University was established in 1998 by an Act of the Andhra Pradesh State Legislature. It is popularly known as NALSAR or NALSAR Hyderabad and is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is accredited with an “A” grade with a score of 3.60 by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).
The University offers full-time UG, PG, doctorate, and diploma programmes in various fields of Law and Management. Apart from the full-time programmes, the University also offers part-time programmes under the NALSAR Directorate of Distance Education (DDE). The full-time courses are offered under nine different centres, namely Centre for Constitutional Law, Centre for Family Laws, Centre for Legal Philosophy, and more. Q: How costly is Nalsar University of Law?

A: Nalsar University is one of the top university for legal studies as specialised in Law in Hyderabad. The Shamirpet, Hyderabad, Telangana-based Institute of Legal Studies provides law programmes at the undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral levels. Additionally, the university offers postgraduate business administration programmes. An applicant must achieve a certain score on the CLAT, CAT, CMAT, XAT, or GMAT in order to be considered for admission at NALSAR. LLM first year fees is 1.75 Lakhs and BA.LLB first year fees is 2.42 Lakhs.

Q: What is the fee for various courses offered by Nalsar University of Law?

A: The cost of the PG Diploma in Nalsar University of Law ranges from 12 to 40K. The cost of the MBA/PGDM programmes in Nalsar University ranges from 4 to 10 L B.A. Tuition for LL.B. (Hons) is 8 L. The total cost of the LL.M. is 65 K. An M.A. course costs 60 K to 1 L, while a B.B.A. course costs 10 L.

Q: What are the fees for LLM programmes at Nalsar University of Law?

A: Nalsar University is a Hyderabad based Law university which offers degrees in the field of law, like BA LLB ( Hons), B.Sc. LLB, LLM etc. Just like other colleges, here too the fee structure strictly depends upon the programme you are opting for, that is, the fee structure for BA LLB (Hons) will be different than that for the LLM students . According to this college's website the fee structure within the LLM will be different for different programs . I e , for LLM General students, the tuition fees will be 1,75,000 with a refundable deposite of 20,000 . whereas LLM in insolvency and bankruptcy law's students will have to pay a tuition fees of 2,25,000 with a refundable deposite of 20,000.

Q: What is the admission process of Nalsar University of Law?

A: Nalsar University of law have courses on undergraduation post-graduation, Diploma and doctorate positions . The admission process is of 4 steps . First step is to register and fill the application form online for the courses other than BA LLB , on the official website of Nalsar University of law . For BA LLB, candidates are required to register through CLAT. Next the candidate need to pay INR 1000 for fees . Second step is to appear for CLAT exam to get admission in BA LLB and LLM programs . Third step is counselling in which qualified candidates will be allotted seats and they have to accept it . Last step is to upload the required documents and submit fees under required time limit .

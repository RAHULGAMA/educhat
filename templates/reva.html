REVA University Highlights 2024

Reva University Bangalore was established under the Reva University Act and is recognised by UGC, the Bar Council of India, COA, the Institution’s Innovation Council, and AICTE. Reva University Yelahanka is accredited by NBA and with B+ grade by NAAC. REVA University is ranked eighth in the State Wise category by Open Magazine Best B-Schools Rankings 2023. REVA University Bangalore, through about 30 schools of study and various departments, offers UG, PG, diploma, certificate, PhD, and other courses to students across the Science and Technology, Commerce, Management Studies, Law, Arts & Humanities, and Performing Arts, and other streams. Q: Is REVA University, Bangalore accredited?
A: Reva University is recognised by UGC (University Grants Commission), the Bar Council of India, COA, the Institution Innovation Council, and AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education). REVA College Bangalore is accredited by the NBA (National Board of Accreditation) and with B+ grade by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council). All the courses offered by REVA University Bangalore are approved by UGC (University Grants Commission) and AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education).
Q: How good is Reva university for MBA?
A: Reva University is considered to be a good institution for pursuing an MBA degree. The university has a faculty of experienced academicians and industry experts who bring practical knowledge and industry insights to the classroom. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of business and management concepts, and it also includes hands-on experience through internships, industry visits, and projects. The university has a strong focus on research, and the faculty members are actively involved in research activities, which helps in creating a conducive learning environment for the students. The university also has collaborations with various national and international organizations, which provides students with opportunities to gain practical experience and exposure to global practices. Reva University has excellent infrastructure facilities, including modern classrooms, a well-stocked library, a computer lab, sports facilities, and a state-of-the-art auditorium. The campus is also Wi-Fi enabled, providing students with easy access to online resources. The university offers placement assistance to students, and the placement record has been consistently good over the years. The university has tie-ups with various national and multinational companies, providing students with opportunities to secure good placements. Overall, Reva University is a good option for students who want to pursue an MBA programme, and it provides a conducive environment for learning and growth.
Q: Is it worth doing MBA from Reva University?
A: Reva University offers MBA courses in a full-time mode via its Reva Business School. The university has been ranked on 147th position by Business Today in the year 2023. The University offers impressive salary packages and fees including well planned curriculum based on the latest market requirements. In the year 2023, many top recruiters, including Accenture, Deloitte, IBM, TCS, etc., visited during the on-campus placement. The overall the highest package was stood at INR 50 LPA.
Q: How to get a seat in MBA at REVA University?
A: To get a seat in MBA at Reva University, students need to fulfil the minimum eligibility criteria in the first place. Every candidate needs to pass graduation in a relevant field or BTech with a minimum aggregate of 50% aggregate (45% for reserved categories) from a recognised university. Admission to MBA at Reva University is entrance-based. Students need to qualify for REVA CET, which is an in-house entrance exam of the university for admission to Management courses. Otherwise, students can also get admission through a valid score in CAT/ MAT/ CMAT entrance exams.

REVA University Admissions Process 2024

Reva University admissions 2024 are open for varios courses. The university offers admission to courses including UG, PG, PhD, several certificate/diploma and PG Diploma programmes. The courses are offered in the fields of Engineering, Architecture, Science & Technology, Commerce, Management Studies, Legal Studies, Arts & Humanities and Performing Arts.

REVA University admissions are based on a valid score in the respective entrance exams. Candidates who wish to pursue BA LLB, MBA, BTech, BBA, BArch, BDes, BCom, BAJMC, BCA, BSc and MCA are selected based on the REVA CET score, which is conducted every year between June to July. However, Reva University also accepts all other major national-level entrance exam scores along with merit obtained in qualifying exams.

Reva University Admission 2024: Eligibility & Selection Criteria Reva University Bangalore admissions to all the UG & PG courses are done based on a valid score of REVA CET. Candidates willing to apply for any of the respective course must fulfil the basic educational requirements along with qualifying for the other selection parameters.

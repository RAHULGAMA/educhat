NLU Delhi Highlights 2023

Established in the year 2008, National Law University, Delhi, also known as NLU Delhi is a state-level university located in the capital of India. Out of the 23 NLUs in the country, NLU Delhi is among the leading ones. The university is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is also a member of AIU. NLU Delhi is accredited with an ‘A’ grade with a score of 3.59 by NAAC and approved by the Bar Council of India (BCI). NLU Delhi is ranked 2 for Law by NIRF 2021. The university offers a total of 11 law courses across two streams at UG, PG and PhD levels. Additionally, NLU Delhi also offers PG diploma, certificate, diploma and bridge courses.

Q: Is NLU Delhi accredited?

A: NLU Delhi is a Grade 'A' University. The score of this University is 3.59 by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and also approved by 'The Bar Council of India' (BCI). The university is among the best law schools in India and holds the 2nd rank in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). The National Law University, the Delhi Centre for Law and Justice, and the Government of India jointly promote legal literacy and legal awareness under the DISHA program of the DoJ.

Q: What is the mode of application for NLU Delhi admission?

A: NLU Delhi conducts its own entrance exam for admission to the All India Law Entrance Test (AILET). It offers admission to undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.
The AILET application form is released in online mode on nationallawuniversitydelhi. in. The application fee for general people is INR 3,500, for PwD its INR 1,500, for SC/ST INR 1,500 and those who are below the poverty line (BPL) are exempt from paying the application fee that is they don't have to pay any application fee to appear in the exam.
The exam mode of AILED is offline. The duration of this exam is 1hour 30minutes.

Q: Does NLU Delhi offer good placements?

A: Yes, NLU Delhi has a decent placement rate, with students from almost all the courses getting placement assistance. During the 2022 placement session, 59 students were placed from the BA LLB (Hons) courses and 43 students were placed from the LLM course. Further, the median package offered during NLU Delhi LLM placements 2022 was INR 10 LPA.

AILET Cut Off 2024

NLU Delhi AILET Cutoff 2024

NLU Delhi admission 2024 to B.A. LL.B. (Hons.) and Master of Law (LL.M.) course is based on AILET scores followed by AILET Counselling which is conducted roundwise. AILET 2024 Counselling 2024 is ongoing. As of now Round 1 of BA LLB is out and Round 2 of LLM is out. The closing AIR for the latest AILET Round 2 for LLM is 36 for the General category. For the OBC category it is 150, for General PwD it is 232 and for ST it is 437.

As per the NLU Delhi cutoff 2024, the closing rank for BA LLB in AILET First Provisional Merit List is 62, whereas for LLM it is 36 in Round 2 for the General category All India candidates. On comparing the latest round ranks for BA LLB, NLU Delhi cutoff 2024 stood at 62. For AILET cut off 2023, BA LLB closed at 62 only for Round 1. Hence, it can be concluded that the university has witnessed no change in ranks for the last two years. For 2022, AILET cutoff for BA LLB was 72 in R1, which is the highest of all the three previous years. For LLM course, NLU Delhi cutoff based on AILET 2024 is 36 in R2, whereas it was 62 in 2023 and 48 in 2022. As per the analysis of last three years, NLU has witnessed a fluctuating trend for LLM in Round 2.

IIT Roorkee Highlights 2024

Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee was established in 1847 and is the seventh IIT recognised as an Institution of National Importance. IIT Roorkee is accredited by NAAC and approved by AICTE and UGC. IIT Roorkee is a public technical research university located in Uttarakhand. IIT Roorkee is the oldest technical institution in Asia that was given university status in 1949 and has ranked 5 by the NIRF 2023 under the Engineering category. The institution is aslo ranked 5 by India Today 2023 under the Engineering (Government), and Engineering categories. IITR offers 14 Undergraduate (UG), 24 Postgraduate (PG), and various other PhD courses through its school and 23 departments across the fields of Engineering, Science, Architecture, Management, and Social Sciences. Globally, IIT Roorkee is ranked 369 by the Q S World University in the category of Top Global Universities Rankings 2024, 14 by the Q S World University Rankings 2024: Southern Asia 2024 and 116 by the Q S World University Rankings: Asia 2024. IITR fees range from INR 12,000 to INR 10.70 lakh. Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee course admission is based on the entrance exam. Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee entrance exams are JEE Main, JEE Advanced, CAT, GATE, IIT JAM, CEED, and NATA. IIT Roorkee cutoff 2023 for the BTech courses ranged between 412 and 20728 for the General All India category students. During IIT Roorkee placements, a placement rate of 100% was recorded. IIT Roorkee average package stood at INR 18.34 LPA. IIT Roorkee has separate boys and girls hostels, one coed hostel, and married couples' hostels. IITR also has a well-equipped library with a collection of over 3.20 lakh documents, online databases, CD-ROMs, and more. The major highlights of IIT Roorkee are tabulated below:

Q: What are the pros and cons of IIT Roorkee for freshers?

IIT Roorkee stands as a prominent institution in India, offering an extensive array of undergraduate, postgraduate, joint degree, exchange, and research programs, totaling to approximately 100. The institution has consistently maintained its reputation, securing the top position in the NIRF Rankings for the year 2023, marking its excellence for the fourth consecutive year. Additionally, it holds the 250th position in the QS World Rankings of 2023.

Renowned for its academic prowess, IIT Roorkee is often the preferred choice among students, particularly those inclined towards engineering programs such as BTech and MTech, drawn by the institution's commitment to delivering quality education and fostering research.

Noteworthy among its attributes is the impressive placement record of IIT Roorkee, with several top-performing students securing packages exceeding 1 Crore.

When it comes to campus life, the allure of IIT Roorkee lies in its captivating natural surroundings. The campus boasts a rich biodiversity, with sightings of deer, blackbucks, and monkeys being a common occurrence.

As for drawbacks, reviews from students of IIT Roorkee do not highlight any significant weaknesses, suggesting a generally favorable experience at the institution.

Q: What are the facilities provided by IIT Roorkee?

The Institute has over three campuses namely a 365-acre campus at Roorkee, a 25-acre campus at Saharanpur, and a 10-acre campus of Greater Noida Extension Centre at Greater Noida. IIT Roorkee has 8,000+ students, hostels, clubs, sports facilities, guest houses, 10 boys' hostels, three girls' hostels, one coed hostel, and six married couples' hostels. The institute has UG, PG, Alaknanda clubs, and various other facilities for the students. IIT Roorkee Admissions Process 2024

IIT Roorkee admission to various programmes is done through national-level entrance exams including JEE Main/ JEE Advanced, IIT JAM and GATE, among others. For admission to the flagship BTech programmes, candidates are selected based on valid JEE-Advanced ranks followed by the counselling process. On the successful completion of the counselling process, candidates are selected for the final seat allotment after the documents verification and tuition fee payment.

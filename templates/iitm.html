IIT Madras Highlights 2024

Identified as an ‘Institute of Eminence', the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras or IITM) was established in 1959 as a public technical and research university. It is the third IIT be set up by the Government of India and is autonomous in nature. Located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, the IIT Madras campus is home to India’s first university-based research park- IITMR. IIT Madras has been consistently ranked 1 by the NIRF Rankings in 2023 for the fourth time in a row. Internationally, IIT Madras was ranked 285 in the QS World University Rankings 2024 and 250 in the QS World University Rankings of 2023. IIT Madras, through its 16 departments, 4 national and 10 institute research centres offer more than 100 full-time/ part-time/ online courses at UG, PG, and doctoral levels across the Engineering, Science, Humanities, and Management. The institute recently launched the country's first online BSc Program in Programming in Data Science. IITM is also known for its Quality Improvement Programme (QIP), which is sponsored by the AICTE. Q: What are the facilities provided by IIT Madras?

IIT Madras provides modern amenities with state-of-the-art infrastructure to its students. IIT Madras is spread over an area of 617 acre and provides various facilities. Some of the facilities include The Centre for Innovation (CFI), hospitals, central library, sports, open air theatre, place to worship, banks, canteens, transport, hostels, etc.

IIT Madras Courses and Fees 2024

Numerous undergraduate, graduate, joint, exchange, and research programs are available at IIT Madras. In the areas of engineering, science, architecture, management, and social sciences, it grants the degrees of BTech, BTech-MTech (Dual Degree), BS-MS (Dual Degree), Integrated MA, MTech, MBA, MSc, MS by Research, and PhD. The flagship four-year BTech programme at IIT Madras is offered. Postgraduate courses like MTech, MBA, MSc, and so forth have a two-year duration. Furthermore, IITM has agreements in place with international universities to provide joint PhD and JDP programs. The institute is also well-known for its AICTE-sponsored Quality Improvement Programme (QIP), the first online Bachelor of Science program in history.

IIT Madras Admissions Process 2024

IIT Madras admission to various programmes is done through national-level entrance exams including JEE Main/ JEE Advanced, IIT JAM and GATE, among others. For admission to the flagship BTech programmes, candidates are selected based on valid JEE-Advanced ranks followed by the counselling process. On the successful completion of the counselling process, candidates are selected for the final seat allotment after the documents verification and tuition fee payment.

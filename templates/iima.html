IIM Ahmedabad Highlights 2024

IIM Ahmedabad is ranked 1st by NIRF 2023 under the Management category, 2nd by Business Today 2023 for MBA, and again 1st by Outlook 2023 under the MBA (Government) category. Globally, the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad is ranked 53rd by the QS WUR Ranking By Subject 2024 and 42 by World University Rankings - Masters In Management 2024. Established in 1961, IIM Ahmedabad, or IIMA is a public Business School located in Vastrapur, Ahmedabad. The institute has been accorded the status of an ‘Institute of National Importance' by MHRD, Government of India.
Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad was the first B-School to receive the EQUIS (European Quality Improvement System) accreditation, by the European Foundation for Management Development (EFMD). This reputed college is approved by UGC. IIM A offers PG, PhD, and other courses to students. These courses are provided across the Management and various other streams.
IIMA offers placement assistance to students. The highest package offered to PGPX students in 2023 was INR 1.08 crore per annum. Top recruiters such as Boston Consulting Group, TCS, Goldman Sachs, and others visited the campus for placements. Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad offers MBA as its flagship course. Q: Is IIM Ahmedabad the best among all the IIMs?

A: IIM Ahmedabad is considered one of the best management institutes in India and is widely regarded as the flagship institute of the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs). However, it is difficult to say whether it is the best among all the IIMs as each institute has its own unique strengths and advantages. Here are some factors to consider when comparing IIM Ahmedabad to other IIMs: Reputation: IIM Ahmedabad is one of the oldest and most reputed management institutes in India and is consistently ranked among the top management institutes in the country. However, other IIMs such as IIM Bangalore and IIM Calcutta are also highly reputed and have their own distinct advantages.

Q: Where are IIM Ahmedabad alumni placed?

Q: How much CAT score is required for MBA at IIM Ahmedabad?

A: IIM Ahmedabad considered two kinds of CAT scores for MBA admission - Qualifying and Final score. Qualifying CAT score is the minimum sectional and overall CAT score used to shortlist students for the Analytical Writing Test (AWT) and Personal Interview (PI) rounds. Candidates above minimum cutoffs in the sectional percentile ranks, the overall percentile rank and having a positive (greater than zero) raw score in all sections of CAT are considered for the next stage. Final CAT score is cutoffs are the basis of which the candidates are called for final selection after the AWT and PI rounds are over. The overall CAT qualifying cutoff for 2022 admission at IIM-A stood at 80 for General category candidates while the final cutoff is considered to stand between 99 and 100 percentile. For other category candidates, this requirement is comparatively low.

IIM Ahmedabad Admissions Process 2024

IIM Ahmedabad admission 2024 is primarily entrance-based. This institute is known for its two-year, full-time, residential flagship PGP (also referred to as MBA) programme that offers admission based on a valid score in CAT. The percentile of CAT for IIM Ahmedabad reached up to 99.6 in the past years which shows how rigorous it is for a student to get admission to this institute. IIM-A admission cycle for PGP commences every year with the start of CAT registrations.
Apart from PGP, IIM A also offers other PG and doctoral degree courses in the field of Management. Candidates are shortlisted for these programmes based on course-specific entrance exams, followed by shortlisting processes which generally comprise a Personal Interview (PI) round. IIM Ahmedabad admission to all courses is done through the scores of various entrance exams, followed by other admission criteria.

IIM Ahmedabad Cutoff 2023

IM Ahmedabad cut off for various PG courses including Post Graduate Programme in Management (PGP), the Executive Post Graduate Programme in Management and the Fellow Programme in Management (FPM) is based on CAT scores. The institute releases two types of CAT cutoffs, namely the Qualifying CAT cutoff and the Final CAT cutoff. Securing a minimum qualifying cutoff just means that the candidate is eligible for the next step in the admission procedure. Also, IIM Ahmedabad final CAT cutoff is always higher than the qualifying cutoff. The Qualifying cutoffs for IIMs mostly remain the same every year. For IIM Ahmedabad admissions 2024, the Qualifying CAT cutoff is set at 80 Percentile, while the sectional cutoffs are established at 70 Percentile for the General category candidates. The Qualifying CAT cutoffs are released for other category candidates as well.

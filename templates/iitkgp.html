IIT Kharagpur Highlights 2024

IIT Kharagpur is one of the renowned institutes present in India. IIT Kharagpur was established in 1951 and is ranked 6th by the NIRF 2023 under the Engineering category. The college is also ranked 4th by India Today 2023 under the Engineering (Government), and Engineering categories. IIT KGP, through various departments and schools of study, offers UG, PG, PhD, and other courses to students across the Management, Law, Engineering, and various other streams. Internationally, IIT Kharagpur is ranked 271 by the QS World University Rankings 2024 under the Top Global Universities category, 5th by the QS World University Rankings: Southern Asia 2024, and 59th by the QS World University Rankings: Asia 2024.
IIT Kharagpur offers competitive placement opportunities to students in top companies. According to media reports, on the first day of IIT Kharagpur placements 2023-24, a total of 700 placement offers were made. It was observed out of all the offers made, 6 offers were made with a package of more than INR 1 crore per annum. The University of Warwick (WMG), National Tsing Hua University (NTHU), Purdue University, and the University of Torino have entered into collaborative agreements with the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur.
IIT KGP is famous for BTech, and MTech courses. The academic session in the college is divided broadly into two semesters. Each has approximately 20 weeks of duration: a Spring Semester (January-April) and an Autumn Semester (July-November). Q: Where are IIT Kharagpur alumni placed?

A: According to unofficial online sources, the majority of IIT Kharagpur alumni are placed in the Engineering sector with a 27% share. The other top sectors that involve a large share of alumni are Business Development and Research.

Q: Does IIT Kharagpur offer equal opportunities to students?

A: Yes, the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur offers equal opportunities to students. Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur provides scholarships to postgraduate, undergraduate, and other programme students as financial assistance. Some of the scholarships offered by the college are the Merit cum Means Scholarship, Free Mess and Pocket Allowance, My Imprint Special Assistance, Incubation Support for Student Enterprises, SGR International Travel Grant, Alumni Scholarships & Awards, Organizational Scholarships & Fellowships, Inspire Scholarship, and others.

Q: Which courses are offered by IIT Kharagpur?

A: The modes of IIT Kharagpur admission are online and offline. Admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate courses is based on merit and scores of entrance tests. At the undergraduate level, IITKGP offers four and five-year B.Tech, B.Sc. Five-year B.Arch and various other courses to students. At the postgraduate level, the IIT Kharagpur offers two and six-year M.Tech, M.Sc. And various other courses to students. Kharagpur IIT also provides Ph.D. and Continuing Education courses to students.

IIT Kharagpur Courses and Fees 2024

IIT Kharagpur offers UG, PG, and Doctoral-level courses in the field of Engineering, Science and Management. Its flagship course is BTech, which is offered across different specialisations. Other than BTech, it offers MTech, MBA, BSc, and various other courses. The tuition fee for all the courses offered here ranges between INR 20,000 to 25 lakh. IIT Kharagpur Admissions Process 2024

IIT Kharagpur admission 2024 is currently open for the MSc, BArch and MTech courses. For its flagship BTech programme, the institute accepts JEE Advanced scores for admission. Candidates are required to qualify JEE Main exam before appearing for JEE Advanced. Additionally, the institute accepts CAT scores for admission to MBA. Apart from its flagship program, IIT Kharagpur offers BSc/BS, Integrated MTech, Integrated MSc, LLM, MBA, and E-MBA, along with PhD programmes to the students. Admission to all IIT Kharagpur courses is based on scores obtained in the entrance examinations such as GATE, IIT JAM, and CAT exams, among others.

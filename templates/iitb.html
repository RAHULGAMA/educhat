IIT Bombay Highlights 2024

Established in 1958, the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (also known as IIT Bombay or IIT Mumbai) is the first public college to have laid its foundation stone with assistance from UNESCO. In 1961, it was declared as an Institute of National Importance by the Parliament of India. It has also been declared a 'Deemed University' and is autonomous in nature. IIT Bombay is ranked 3 by NIRF 2023 under the Engineering category, 2 by India Today 2023 under the Engineering category, and 2 by India Today 2023 under the Engineering (Government) category.
Internationally, IIT Bombay is ranked 149 by the QS World University Rankings 2024 under the Top Global Universities category and 172 in the QS World Rankings 2023. IIT Bombay offers degree and dual degree programmes at the UG, PG and doctorate levels. The institute offers 80+ courses under 16 departments in the stream of Engineering, Technology, Science and Management. The applicants are chosen through national-level tests conducted by various authorities, such as JEE Advanced, GATE, etc.
With a lifespan of more than 60 years, IITB campus has grown to include 16 departments, 30+ centres and three schools to offer more than 80 courses in the stream of Engineering and Technology, Science and Management at UG, PG and doctoral levels. Much like other IITs, the flagship course of IIT Bombay is BTech with a total intake of 1,558 students. A few of the important highlights regarding IITB are mentioned in the table below:

Q: Can I get MTech in IIT Bombay with GATE?

A: Yes, GATE score is required to get admission to IIT Bombay course. However, aspirants who have not qualified GATE can also get admission based on written test conducted by the institute. The shortlisted candidates are further called for a personal interview round. In order to be eligible to apply for the course, an aspirant must have a BE/ BTech/ BSc/ Bachelor of Architecture and Bachelor of Planning/ MSc/ MCA/ MBBS/ MPharm/ BPharm/ BDS/ degree recognised equivalent to BE/ BTech with a minimum of 60% aggregate (55% aggregate for SC/ ST/ PwD category) earned through Associate Membership Examinations conducted by recognised professional bodies to apply for MTech course.

Q: What is IIT Bombay famous for?

A: IIT Bombay is a prominent Engineering institute in India. The institute is famous for several things, such as its academic excellence. IIT Bombay has been consistently ranked among the top institutes in India for offering good Engineering courses. The institute has a challenging and rigorous curriculum which covers a wide range of Engineering disciplines. The institute has a vast and diverse alumni network spread across globe. The institute has a vibrant student life with a range of extracurricular activities. Overall, IIT Bombay is famous for its academic excellence, alumni network, beautiful campus and vibrant student life.

Q: What are the pros and cons of studying in IIT Bombay?

A: There are several pros and cons for studying at IIT Bombay. Candidates can read the below information to know:
Pros:
IIT Bombay is the top institute for technology in India. Best place for over all development. Huge attractive packages in top MNCs for many students. The institute has collaborations with leading organizations, which provide good opportunities to students for internships, placements and live projects.
Cons:
The institute has an intense academic pressure and high expectations can be overwhelming, leading to stress and anxiety. Maintaining a balance in academics, extracurricular activities, and personal life can be challenging, requiring strong time management skills. Being surrounded by brilliant peers can lead to feelings of inadequacy or insecurity, requiring a strong sense of self-worth. Henceforth, whether IIT Bombay is a good fit for you depends on your individual priorities and preferences. Weighing both the pros and cons, considering your academic goals, personality, and adaptability, will help you make an informed decision.

IIT Bombay Admissions Process 2024

IIT Bombay course admission 2024 is both merit and entrance-based. IITB accepts national-level entrance exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, GATE, etc., for admission to specific courses. The institute offers over 86 courses at UG, PG, and PhD levels in the streams of Engineering, Technology, Science, and Management. Besides, IIT Bombay also offers short-term certificate online courses like Object-oriented Programming, Programming Basics, etc. IIT Bombay cutoff will be considered for the final selection of the candidates. As per NIRF 2023 Rankings, IIT Bombay ranked at '7' under the 'Innovation' Category. This is a newly introduced Engineering category of NIRF.

The classrooms of IIT Bombay are bestowed with audio-visual aids along with digital smart boards to promote innovative and interactive learning.
